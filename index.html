Nilam Sherpa

Principal | Founder

With a proven track record in educational leadership, Nilam Sherpa has 20 years of experience shaping the future of young minds. She is a dynamic and visionary leader who believes in cultivating an inclusive and engaging learning environment. Known for Headmaster, Nilam Sherpa is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, nurturing creativity, and instilling values that prepare students for success in a rapidly evolving world.

Through collaborative efforts with staff, parents, and the community, she continues to make a lasting impact on the educational journey of every student under her guidance.
